The Story of Bob the dog

Once upon a time, there was a dog named Bob. Bob had to use the bathroom. Bob was looking for bathrooms, and eventually he saw a stop sign. Bob peed on the sign. It was much needed relief. "Now I will run around" Bob said. Bob ran around and around until he got tired "I'm tired" says Bob. Bob went back home and went through his dog door Bob then curled up on his bed and fell asleep.
